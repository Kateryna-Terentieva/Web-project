Why I have started traveling

I have started traveling more once I have joined Kiwi.com as a travel company. I found out how many routes and destinations exist and how often people all over the world travel and I felt I need that too and need to have my life more colourful.

My very first trip was to Poland since I have been learning Polish as school and still remeber something. Wroclaw was the first city to visit and I do not regret about this since this city is very cozy and warm with loooooots of places to visit.
